## Day 46 – Airline Delay Analysis ✈️
# Project Overview

This project analyzes airline delay data to identify the major causes of flight delays, airport performance issues, 
and operational bottlenecks. Using Power BI, the dataset was cleaned, analyzed, and transformed into interactive dashboards
to generate actionable business insights.

# Objectives
Identify airports with the highest delays
Analyze major causes of delays
Study weather-related impacts
Compare delayed vs total flights
Generate recommendations to reduce delays

## Key Insights
Certain airports contribute significantly to overall delays
Weather and NAS delays heavily affect airline operations
Late aircraft arrivals are a major operational issue
Delay percentages vary across airports and months

# Business Recommendations
Improve airport operational efficiency
Enhance weather prediction systems
Reduce aircraft turnaround time
Improve flight scheduling during peak periods
Strengthen passenger communication systems

# Conclusion

This analysis provides valuable insights into airline operational performance and helps identify the key factors contributing to flight delays. 
The dashboard enables better decision-making for improving airline efficiency and passenger experience.
