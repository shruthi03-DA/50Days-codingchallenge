## Task 1: Total Expenses
What we did: Added up all expense categories: rent, food, transport, and entertainment.
Code: total_expenses = rent + food + transport + entertainment
Logic: This gives a single number that represents total monthly outflow.
Result: Total Expenses: 27000

## Task 2: Savings Calculation
What we did: Subtracted total expenses from salary to find what’s left.
Code: savings = salary - total_expenses
Logic: Savings = Income – Expenditure. This is the core of any budget.
Result: Savings: 23000

## Task 3: Expense Comparison
What we did: Used comparison operators to check relationships between expense types
Code: rent > food   Is rent costlier than food?
transport < entertainment Is transport cheaper than entertainment?
Logic: Boolean checks help flag which categories are taking more budget share.
Result:javascriptIs rent greater than food? True
Is transport less than entertainment

## Task 4: Financial Condition Check
What we did: Categorized the savings amount into 3 bands using if-elif-else.
Logic:If savings > 10000 → “Good Saving”If savings between 5000 and 10000 → “Average Saving”Else → “Low Saving”
Result: Financial Status: Good Saving

## Task 5: Bonus Update
What we did: Applied a 10% bonus to the salary and recalculated.
Code: salary += salary _ 0.10 which is the same as salary = salary _ 1.10
Logic: This simulates a salary hike or annual bonus scenario to see the new income.
Result: Updated Salary after Bonus: 55000.0
