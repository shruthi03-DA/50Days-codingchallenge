Day 1 – Excel Data Analyst Challenge
Problem Statement
Analyze retail sales data to understand regional performance and identify top-performing products.

Scenario
You are a Junior Data Analyst working for a retail company. The sales team has shared raw sales data and asked you to analyze regional sales performance and product revenue trends.

Tasks Completed
Calculated Total Revenue by Region
Analyzed Revenue by Product Category
Identified Top 5 Performing Products by Revenue
Built an interactive Excel dashboard using slicers
Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts
Slicers
Dashboard Features
Total Revenue by Region – Column Chart
Total Revenue by Product Category – Pie Chart
Top 5 Performing Products – Bar Chart
Interactive slicers for Region, Category, and Product
Dataset
The dataset contains retail sales transaction data including:

Region
Product

Quantity
Unit Price
Key Insights
Some regions generate significantly higher revenue compared to others.
A few product categories contribute the majority of total sales.
The top 5 products account for a large share of the total revenue.
