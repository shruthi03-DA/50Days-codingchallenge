# Day 43 – E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce retail dataset to identify why company profits are decreasing despite increasing sales.

The analysis focuses on:
- Discounts vs Profit
- High-loss product categories
- Regional performance
- Shipping and delivery inefficiencies
- Business recommendations

---

# Problem Statement

An online retail company is facing declining profits despite increasing sales.

The objective is to:
- Clean the dataset
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Create visualizations
- Find business insights
- Recommend improvement strategies

---

# Dataset Columns

| Column Name | Description |
|---|---|
| Order_ID | Unique order identifier |
| Customer_ID | Customer identifier |
| Order_Date | Date of purchase |
| Product_Category | Product category |
| Discount | Discount percentage |
| Sales | Total sales amount |
| Profit | Profit earned/loss |
| Region | Sales region |
| Shipping_Cost | Delivery shipping cost |
| Delivery_Time | Delivery duration |

# Key Insights

## 1. Discounts Reduced Profitability
Higher discount percentages were frequently associated with low or negative profits.

## 2. Certain Categories Generated Losses
Some product categories had high sales but poor profit margins.

## 3. Regional Delivery Delays
Regions with longer delivery times showed reduced profitability.

## 4. High Shipping Costs Impacted Margins
Operational costs affected overall business performance.

---

# Business Recommendations

## Optimize Discount Strategy
- Reduce excessive discounting
- Apply discounts strategically

## Improve Logistics
- Reduce shipping costs
- Improve delivery efficiency

## Focus on Profitable Products
- Promote high-margin categories
- Reduce loss-making inventory

## Monitor Regional Performance
- Improve operations in weak-performing regions

---


# Conclusion

The analysis revealed that aggressive discounting and operational inefficiencies were the major reasons for declining profits. Strategic pricing and logistics improvements can significantly improve overall profitability.
